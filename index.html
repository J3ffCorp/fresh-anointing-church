About Our Church

Fresh Anointing Church exists to share the Gospel, strengthen families, and create a place where everyone can encounter God.

Our Mission

To preach Christ, disciple believers, and serve the community with love, truth, and compassion.

Our Vision

To raise people of faith who walk boldly in purpose and reflect God’s power in everyday life.

Our Community

We welcome individuals, couples, youth, and families looking for a church home centered on worship and growth.
